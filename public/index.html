From the Desk of Steve Jobs

It has recently been brought to the attention of the Internets that one Steve Jobs, the CEO of a revolutionary, magical, multibillion-dollar company had taken to answering all of his emails himself. Not just the ones involving trillion-dollar acquisitions or Microsoft TabletPC launches mind you, but even emails from regular joes like you and me. People who would ordinarily not warrant even a nanosecond of his time. Clearly, something is afoot. After all, you don't seriously believe that someone like Mr. Jobs would give two shits about you and your iPhone tethering issues, do you? Of course not! Clearly, this is all the work of Mr. Jobs' faithful dog Fireball, and the god-like auto-complete on Steve's trusty iPad!

Instructions

To play, send out as many emails as you can while Steve is sleeping!

Don't worry about making sense, the iPad knows what you need to say. Be careful though - if Steve wakes up and catches you, he'll be madder than if you had caught you selling prototypes to Gizmodo! (Press the ESC key to turn off the iPad so he can go back to sleep.)
